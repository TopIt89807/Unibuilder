Jobsite Information
The Job Status allows you To mark a jobsite As Closed, which places it in an archived status. This will remove the jobsite from your everyday list, but will keep it archived for your records.

Job Schedule Information
The Workdays allow you to set the working days of the week for your project schedule.
Selecting a default color for a particular jobsite can be very helpful for viewing the calendar in the ALL JOBSITES view. All schedule items associated with a particular jobsite will appear in the selected color.
Selecting a default color for a particular jobsite will also automatically default the background color for all newly added schedule items on the calendar.
